If the detectives and criminals act exciting shows together, then, writers, scriptwriters and derictors are the Ma Liang's Divine Pen of the show, who definitely worth us respect. There are several genius level creators below as you can watch.

Christopher Nolan

Director, screenwriter, photographer

Born in London, England in 1970, he has directed or directed films such as Memento, Batman series, The Prestige, Inception, Interstellar, Dunkirk, Tenet, and Oppenheimer. Won many international awards including Oscars and Golden Globe Awards.

Agatha Christie

Writer

Born in Torquay, Devon, England in 1890, his major works include The Mysterious Affair of Roger, Murder on the Orient Express, The ABC Murders, Death on the Nile, and Then There Were None, Evil in the Sun, and The Mousetrap.

Lou Ye (娄烨)

Director, screenwriter, producer, actor, editor

Born in Shanghai, China in 1965, his major works include Weekend Lovers, Dangerous Girl, Suzhou River, Purple Butterfly, Summer Palace, Intoxicated by Spring Night, Floating City, Massage, and A Cloud Made of Rain in the Wind..

Miyabe Miyuki (宫部みゆき)

Writer

Born in Tokyo in 1960, she was elected "Japan's Most Popular Female Writer" for 11 consecutive years. She is known as "a miracle in the history of Japanese literature". Her major works include Ryūmān, The Copycat, and Solomon's Perjury.
